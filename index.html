Common Questions

How long does the production process take?

Production timelines vary based on design complexity, fabric availability, and order size. For in-stock fabrics, the process typically takes around 30 days, while custom-made fabrics may require up to 90 days.

Can you assist with design and product development?

Yes, DDG provides basic graphic design services to refine your concepts. For more comprehensive design work, we offer additional services at cost.

Do you handle shipping to multiple locations?

Absolutely. Our logistics team can coordinate shipments to multiple destinations, ensuring efficient and timely delivery.

What types of garments can you produce?

We manufacture a wide range of apparel, from casual & activewear to workwear & corporate uniforms. If you have specific requirements, please don't hesitate to reach out so we can discuss customisation options.

Do you offer sustainable or eco-friendly production options?

Yes, we can source sustainable fabrics and implement eco-conscious production methods upon request. Let us know your sustainability goals, and we'll tailor the process accordingly.

Why choose DDG Projects?

As the manufacturer, we provide direct production with full control over quality, timelines, and costs—offering a transparent and reliable process from start to finish.
